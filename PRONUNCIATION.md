# PRONUNCIATION.md

The name of this project is written as:

**Nanox**
**拿錄**

Core sound: **na.noks**
(closed, dry, no trailing vowels)

Each language is encouraged to follow its own phonological habits ---
as long as the sound finally collapses to the same closed core.

---

## 한국어 (Korean)

* 錄은 반드시 두음법칙을 강제로 적용한다.
* "녹을 타다"의 **녹**으로 읽는다.
* 발음: **나-녹-스**

(끝소리를 늘이지 말고 딱 끊어서 닫는다.)

---

## 中文 (Cantonese / Wu preferred)

* **拿錄 = na-lok / na-ngok**

（不要像普通话那样把尾音拉平，要用南方方言那种干脆收尾的读法。）

## English

(Pronounce the consonant cluster, not the letter name.)

## Русский (Russian)

(Произносить как сочетание согласных, а не как название буквы.)

## Deutsch (German)

(Den Vokal nicht ziehen, sondern mit den Konsonanten hart abschließen.)

## भारतीय भाषाएँ / Indic scripts (pick your weapon)

Rule is boringly strict:

* Keep **na.noks**
* Keep the final cluster as **-ks**
* Do **not** soften, do **not** leak a vowel after **k**
* Do **not** "simplify" it into something comfy

(Choose one script. Your tongue must still obey **-ks**.)

> סגור בבירור ב־**k** ואחר כך **s**, בלי להוסיף תנועה.

## བོད་སྐད་ (Tibetan)

> མཐའ་མའི་སྐད་ཡིག་རིང་དུ་མ་བཏང་།
> **k** དང་དེའི་རྗེས་ **s** ཡིས་གསལ་བར་སྒོ་བརྒྱབ།

## မြန်မာဘာသာ (Burmese)

> **k** နောက်မှာ **s** နဲ့ တင်းကျပ်စွာ ပိတ်ပါ၊ သရသံ မထည့်ပါနှင့်။

## Fijian (Melanesia)

> Kakua ni vakabalavutaka na vaueli e muri.
> Katia vakadodonu ena **k** kei na **s**, kakua ni vakuria e dua tale na vaueli.
